## Purpose

## 1. Definitions

1.1 **"Confidential Information"** means all information (whether oral, written, or in any other form) disclosed by or on behalf of the Disclosing Party to the Receiving Party in connection with the Purpose, including but not limited to: business plans, financial data, customer lists, technical data, trade secrets, know-how, product designs, pricing information, and any analyses, compilations, or reports derived from any of the foregoing.

1.2 **"Representatives"** means the Receiving Party's directors, officers, employees, professional advisers (including legal and accounting advisers), and [[permitted_representatives]], in each case on a need-to-know basis.

## 2. Obligation of Confidence

2.1 The Receiving Party undertakes to:
   (a) hold the Confidential Information in strict confidence;
   (b) not disclose the Confidential Information to any third party except as expressly permitted under clause 3;
   (c) use the Confidential Information solely for the Purpose and for no other purpose; and
   (d) apply no less than a reasonable degree of care to protect the Confidential Information.

## 3. Permitted Disclosures

3.1 The Receiving Party may disclose Confidential Information to its Representatives who:
   (a) have a genuine need to know the Confidential Information for the Purpose; and
   (b) are bound by obligations of confidentiality no less stringent than those contained in this Agreement.

3.2 The Receiving Party shall be responsible for any breach of this Agreement by its Representatives.

## 4. Exclusions

4.1 The obligations in clause 2 shall not apply to any Confidential Information that:
   (a) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party;
   (b) was in the Receiving Party's lawful possession prior to disclosure, as demonstrated by written records;
   (c) is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of or reference to the Confidential Information;
   (d) is lawfully received from a third party without restriction on disclosure; or
   (e) is required to be disclosed by law, regulation, or court order, provided that the Receiving Party promptly notifies the Disclosing Party (where permitted) and cooperates with any attempt to obtain a protective order.

## 5. Duration of Obligation

5.1 The obligations of confidentiality under this Agreement shall continue for a period of [[confidentiality_duration_years]] years from the date of this Agreement.

5.2 Notwithstanding clause 5.1, the obligations of confidentiality in respect of Confidential Information constituting a **trade secret** shall continue [[trade_secrets_indefinite]] (if true: indefinitely / if false: for [[confidentiality_duration_years]] years).

## 6. Return or Destruction

6.1 Upon the Disclosing Party's written request, the Receiving Party shall promptly:
   (a) return all copies of the Confidential Information to the Disclosing Party; or
   (b) destroy all copies of the Confidential Information and certify in writing that destruction has been completed.

6.2 The Receiving Party may retain one copy of the Confidential Information solely for regulatory and legal compliance purposes, subject to continuing confidentiality obligations.

## 7. No Licence or Implied Rights

7.1 Nothing in this Agreement grants the Receiving Party any licence, right, or interest in or to any intellectual property rights of the Disclosing Party. All Confidential Information remains the property of the Disclosing Party.

## 8. Remedies

8.1 The Receiving Party acknowledges that damages alone would be an inadequate remedy for any breach of this Agreement. The Disclosing Party shall be entitled, without prejudice to any other rights or remedies, to seek injunctive or other equitable relief for any threatened or actual breach.

## 9. Whistleblowing / PIDA Protection

9.1 **Nothing in this Agreement prevents the Receiving Party from making a protected disclosure within the meaning of section 43A of the Employment Rights Act 1996 (Public Interest Disclosure Act 1998), or from reporting any matter to a regulatory or law enforcement authority.**

## 10. Entire Agreement

10.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties concerning the subject matter and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, and representations.

## 11. Variation

11.1 No variation of this Agreement shall be valid unless made in writing and signed by both Parties.

## 12. Waiver

12.1 A failure or delay by either Party to exercise any right under this Agreement shall not operate as a waiver of that right.

## 13. Severability

13.1 If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

## 14. Notices

14.1 All notices under this Agreement shall be in writing and delivered by email or by hand to the address set out at the head of this Agreement, or to such other address as a Party may notify in writing.

## 15. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

15.1 This Agreement and any disputes arising out of or in connection with it shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of [[governing_law]].

15.2 The Parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of [[governing_law]].

## 16. Counterparts

## 17. Third Party Rights
